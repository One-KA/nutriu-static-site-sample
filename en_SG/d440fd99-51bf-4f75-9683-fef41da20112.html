Crispy salmon cubes

Crispy salmon cubes

Tip: You can also prepare crispy fish cubes with other types of solid fish fillets.

Servings Preparation time 20.0 minutes Cooking time: 12.0 minutes

Nutrition

  • Energy: 512.5 calories
  • Carbs: 29.3 grams
  • Protein: 42.7 grams
  • Fat: 24.7 grams

Ingredients

  • 1.0 piece egg
  • 50.0 gram cornflakes
  • 350.0 gram salmon fillet
  • 1.0 teaspoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1.0 pinch salt
  • 1.0 tablespoon flour
  • 1.0 piece fennel (bulb)
  • 1.0 teaspoon olive oil
  • 20.0 milliliter lemon juice
  • 1.0 pinch pepper

Directions

  • 1. First, crumble the cornflakes and, if necessary, remove the skin and bones from the salmon fillet.
  • 2. Wash the fennel, cut it in half and remove the stalk. Then, cut it into thin strips or slices. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Mix in the rest of the lemon juice and olive oil and allow to marinate for approx. 10 minutes.
  • 3. Serve the crispy salmon cubes with the fennel and drizzle with a little balsamic vinegar.
  • 4. Cut the salmon fillet into approximately 3 x 3 cm cubes. Season with half of the lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Coat with the flour, beaten egg, and cornflakes. Bake in the Airfryer at the indicated temperature and time.
