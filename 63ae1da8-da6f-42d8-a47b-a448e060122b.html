Rhubarb tarte

Rhubarb tarte

Positioning the rhubarb stalks in a parallel way creates a nice visual effect. Rhubarb is not available all year round, make use of it while you can!

Servings Preparation time 20.0 minutes Cooking time: 20.0 minutes

Ingredients

  • 200.0 rice drink
  • 1.0 baking powder
  • 1.0 lemon juice
  • 2.0 water
  • 1.0 salt
  • 40.0 vanilla pudding powder
  • 1.0 egg, medium size
  • 5.0 powdered sugar
  • 200.0 flour
  • 300.0 rhubarb
  • 75.0 cane sugar
  • 125.0 butter

Directions

  • Carefully remove from the baking accessory and serve!
  • Place the baking accessory into the basket of the Airfryer and bake at the indicated temperature and time.
  • Add custard layer, place rhubarb, sprinkle with lemon juice and icing sugar.
  • Cut the butter into small cubes. Use a food processor to process flour, baking powder, sugar and salt with the butter cubes into bread crumb consistency. Add egg and water and continue to process into a dough, leave to cool in the fridge.
  • Roll out dough and cover the bottom and edges of the baking accessory.
  • Cut rhubarb into long strip, that fit the baking accessory.
  • Butter baking form and dust with flour
  • Prepare custard by heating up 80 % of the rice drink in a pan on the stove. Mix the custard powder with the rest of the rice drink and pour into the hot fluid. Stir well and continue to heat until the custard thickens, remove from the heat. Tip: You can replace rice drink with normal milk
